AI Title Enhancement for Thumbnails

**Project:** ViewBait.app
**Link:** https://viewbait.app

**The app**
ViewBait is a thumbnail studio for YouTube creators. The text on your thumbnail is a first-class input, not an SEO footnote. Title Hook Lab helps you sharpen that hook before you spend credits on visuals.

**The problem**
Creators typed a flat topic like "budget camera review" and hit Generate. The image was fine. The hook was not. They burned credits fixing visuals when the title on the canvas was the real problem.

**The solution**
We built **Title Hook Lab**. One click turns a rough topic into three clickable title suggestions. Pick one or more, then generate with that exact text on the thumbnail.

**How it works**
Hit Enhance on the thumbnail text field. The server returns three variations built for curiosity without misleading the viewer. Select the lines you like and generate. Each selected title becomes its own thumbnail with the same face, style, and colors, so you test the hook, not the whole design. Available on Starter tier and above, enforced in the UI and API.

**The result**
Copy testing happens in seconds before image credits spend. Creators enhance, pick two titles, generate both, and compare in the live feed. Fewer "wrong hook" regenerations, better A/B decisions.

**Bottom line**
When text and image ship together, improve the cheap layer first. That ordering saves money and makes comparison actually meaningful. Try it at viewbait.app.
