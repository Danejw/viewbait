# Audience brief: How ViewBait Creates Your Thumbnails

## Reader

YouTube creators, short-form video makers, and anyone curious about AI thumbnails who uses or might try ViewBait.

## Moment

They want to understand what happens when they click Generate, or they are comparing ViewBait to generic AI image tools.

## Intent

**Know:** Understand the generation experience, what inputs matter, and why results feel more consistent than a chat prompt.

## Vocabulary

- Thumbnail, YouTube, face library, style, variations, credits
- 16:9, Shorts, expression, pose, gallery

## Obstacles

- Fear of needing prompt engineering skills
- Uncertainty about credits when generation fails
- Confusion about faces vs style references

## Desired outcome

Feel confident using the studio: what to fill in, what to expect, and how to get better results on the next video.

## Best format

Evergreen explainer for creators (product tour + practical tips).

## Best channels

Help center, blog, onboarding email, social teasers.

## Scorecard

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary job** | Explain the thumbnail generation experience clearly |
| **Primary KPI** | Studio signups or first generation from article readers |
| **Supporting KPIs** | Time on page, help ticket reduction about credits/faces |
| **Review cadence** | When studio UI or pricing changes |
