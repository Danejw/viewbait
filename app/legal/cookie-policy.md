## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how ViewBait ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and application. ## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

### Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function properly. These cookies enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of essential cookies.

### Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your preferences) and provide enhanced, personalized features.

## Cookies We Use

The following table provides details about the cookies we use:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|------------|---------|----------|------|
| `sb-{project}-auth-token` | Authentication session management. Maintains your login state and enables secure access to your account. | Session-based (refreshed automatically) | Essential |
| `sb-{project}-auth-token-code-verifier` | Authentication session management. Used for secure authentication flow (PKCE - Proof Key for Code Exchange). | Session-based (refreshed automatically) | Essential |

### Authentication Cookies

We use Supabase Auth to manage user authentication. The authentication cookies listed above are essential for:

- **Maintaining your login session**: These cookies keep you logged in while you use our service
- **Security**: They help protect your account from unauthorized access
- **Session management**: They enable secure communication between your browser and our servers

These cookies are automatically refreshed during your session to maintain security. They are deleted when you log out or when your session expires.

**Note**: The `{project}` placeholder in the cookie names represents your Supabase project identifier, which is unique to our application.

## Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to:

- Remember your preferences and settings
- Store temporary data needed for service functionality
- Maintain application state

Local storage data is stored on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except as part of normal application functionality.

For more information about how we use local storage, please see section 3.11 of our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

## Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party analytics cookies or advertising trackers. All cookies we use are either essential for service operation or functional for user preferences.

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Please keep in mind that removing or blocking cookies can impact your user experience and parts of our website may no longer be fully accessible.

Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, if you choose to decline essential cookies, you may not be able to use certain features of our service, including the ability to log in.

Here are links to help you manage cookies in popular browsers:

- [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
- [Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac)

### Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can manage cookies through your device settings. Please refer to your device manufacturer's instructions for more information.
